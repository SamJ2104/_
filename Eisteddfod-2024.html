Eisteddfod

13 Dec 2022
In preparation for the upcoming Eisteddfod event on March 1st, 2024, we kindly request all students to wear red attire to school on that day. The Eisteddfod promises to be an exciting celebration of talent and creativity, and we encourage everyone to participate in the spirit of the occasion by sporting red clothing.
